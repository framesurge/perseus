Perseus

The Rust framework for the modern web.


Get Started GitHub

Perseus is a web development framework built entirely in Rust, designed to empower efficient development of the next generation of lightning-fast sites for the modern web. It comes with server-side rendering, static site generation, internationalization, and inferred routing built-in, letting you build even the most complex sites in a breeze.


  • Static generation

    Pre-render pages at build-time before you've even had any visits!

  • Server-side rendering

    Render pages on the server to reduce the load on the client.

  • Internationalization

    Out-of-the-box support for building your app in multiple languages.

  • Incremental generation

    Render pages on-demand and cache them for future use.

  • Revalidation

    Rebuild pages automatically without lifting a finger.

  • CLI

    Comes with a friendly interface to accelerate your development pace.

  • Inferred routing

    You do your app, Perseus does the rest automatically.

  • App shell

    Get all the benefits of a single-page app without any of the downsides.

  • Single-command deployment

    Take your apps to production with one command.

  • Static exporting

    Deploy your apps without needing a server.



Building a Perseus App:

  1. Install Perseus: cargo install perseus-cli
  2. Build your awesome app with Sycamore
  3. Serve your app locally: perseus serve
  4. Deploy your app to a single directory: perseus deploy
  5. Upload that to a server!
